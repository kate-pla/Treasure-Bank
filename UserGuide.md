# Hardware

**First Arduino**
- LCD Screen
- 8 buttons
- Bread board
- Male to Female wires

**Second Arduino**
- Photoresistor
- Transistor
- 8ohm 3w speaker
- SD card Module
- Yellow LED
- Red LED
- Male to Female Wires
